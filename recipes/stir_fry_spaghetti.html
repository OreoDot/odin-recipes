Stir Fry Spaghetti

Road To Making A Stir Fry Spaghetti

The Stir Fry Spaghettirecipe is a unique recipe created to meet the need of a Spaghetti lover

It is easy to make and can be compounded with different inridients for desired output

Ingridients

  • Quality Spaghetti
  • Chilli Pepper
  • Groungnut Oil
  • Chicken/Beef Stock
  • Desired Seasonings
  • Corned Beef

Steps

  1. Parboil the Spaghetti
  2. Make Your desired stock
  3. Make a pepper paste using Onion and Tomatoes
  4. Bleech the oil with Regulated Heat
  5. Add the (pepper paste, stock and Desired Seasonings)
  6. After a few minutes, add your parboiled Spaghetti
  7. When the Spaghetti is almost done, add the Corned Beef and Green Peas
  8. Allow to Seamer for some minutes
  9. Voila! You have your Sumptuous Stir Fry Spaghetti
