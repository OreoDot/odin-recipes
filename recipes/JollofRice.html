Jollof Rice

Road To Making A Sumptuous Jollof Rice

The Jollof Rice recipe is a unique recipe created to meet the need of a rice lover

It is easy to make and can be compounded with different inridients for desired output

Ingridients

  • Quality Rice
  • Chilli Pepper
  • Groungnut Oil
  • Chicken/Beef Stock
  • Desired Seasonings
  • Green Peas
  • Corned Beef

Steps

  1. Parboil the Rice
  2. Make Your desired stock
  3. Make a pepper paste using Onion and Tomatoes
  4. Bleech the oil with Regulated Heat
  5. Add the (pepper paste, stock and Desired Seasonings)
  6. After a few minutes, add your parboiled rice
  7. When the Rice is almost done, add the Corned Beef and Green Peas
  8. Allow to Seamer for some minutes
  9. Voila! You have your Sumptuous Jollof Rice
